FAQ

Funkprism will take place on January 27-28, 2024 at Rose bowl,California. Festival ground map will be updated closer to the event dates
WILL I BE ABLE TO SWITCH MY TICKET TO A DIFFERENT CATEGORY? No, tickets once purchased cannot be upgraded/changed in terms of category
HOW DO I GET A REFUND IF I CAN’T MAKE IT? Tickets for the festival are not transferable or cancellable. In case of queries, write to us here.
IS THERE AN ALL-AREA-ACCESS TICKET WITH AN ARTIST MEET & GREET? There are no all-area-access passes.As already mentioned,VIP tickets get to watch singers right infront of them.Cams are allowed and they are also allowed to give gifts .Perks & privileges on special category tickets are mentioned in the ticket descriptions.
DO I NEED TO BE VACCINATED TO GET INTO THE VENUE? In the best interest of fans and staff, we encourage everyone to follow COVID-19 protocols mandated by local governments. By purchasing tickets to this event, you agree to abide by the health and safety measures in effect at the time of the event.
IS THE EVENT KID-FRIENDLY? Kids aged 0-5 can enter the festival for free. The event is open to all ages. Funkprism is a kid-friendly, family-friendly festival around the world - as long as there’s a guardian for anyone below the age of 16, there’s admission! Guardians will need to sign a waiver to enter the festival with their child
IS THERE AN AGE LIMIT FOR TICKET PURCHASES? Ages 5 - 16 will need their tickets to be purchased by their assigned guardian. They need to buy a ticket matching their accompanying guardian’s ticket category
WHAT IS THE DURATION OF THE FESTIVAL? The festival will take place between January 27th - 28th, 2024, spanning two days. Festival schedule & artist/stage timings will be announced closer to the festival date.
IS THERE PARKING AVAILABLE AT THE VENUE? General parking is currently not available at the venue. Parking perks on special category tickets are available. More details will be shared soon.
